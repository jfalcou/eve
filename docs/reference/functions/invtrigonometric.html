**Expressive Vector Engine** (insert ../../crumbs.html here) # Inverse Trigonometric Functions These functions provide scalar and SIMD version of the inverse trigonometric functions in radians, degrees and $\pi$ multiples. ## Functions **Convenience header:** ### Radians [`eve::acos`](invtrigonometric/acos.html) : computes the arccosine of its parameter [`eve::acot`](invtrigonometric/acot.html) : computes the arccotangentof of its parameter [`eve::acsc`](invtrigonometric/acsc.html) : computes the arccosecant of its parameter [`eve::asec`](invtrigonometric/asec.html) : computes the arcsecant of its parameter [`eve::asin`](invtrigonometric/asin.html) : computes the arcsine of its parameter [`eve::atan2`](invtrigonometric/atan2.html) : computes the arctangent, using signs to determine quadrants, of its parameter [`eve::atan`](invtrigonometric/atan.html) : computes the arctangent of its parameter ### Degrees [`eve::acosd`](invtrigonometric/acosd.html) : computes the arccosine of its parameter [`eve::acotd`](invtrigonometric/acotd.html) : computes the arccotangent of its parameter [`eve::acscd`](invtrigonometric/acscd.html) : computes the arccosecant of its parameter [`eve::asecd`](invtrigonometric/asecd.html) : computes the arcsecant of its parameter [`eve::asind`](invtrigonometric/asind.html) : computes the arcsine of its parameter [`eve::atan2d`](invtrigonometric/atan2d.html) : computes the arctangent, using signs to determine quadrants, of its parameter [`eve::atand`](invtrigonometric/atand.html) : computes the arctangent of its parameter ### Multiple of $\pi$ [`eve::acospi`](invtrigonometric/acospi.html) : computes the arccosine of its parameter [`eve::acotpi`](invtrigonometric/acotpi.html) : computes the arccotangent of its parameter [`eve::acscpi`](invtrigonometric/acscpi.html) : computes the arccosecant of its parameter [`eve::asecpi`](invtrigonometric/asecpi.html) : computes the arcsecant of its parameter [`eve::asinpi`](invtrigonometric/asinpi.html) : computes the arcsine of its parameter [`eve::atan2pi`](invtrigonometric/atan2pi.html) : computes the arctangent, using signs to determine quadrants, of its parameter [`eve::atanpi`](invtrigonometric/atanpi.html) : computes the arctangent
