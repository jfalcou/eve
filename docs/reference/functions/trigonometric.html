**Expressive Vector Engine** (insert ../../crumbs.html here) # Trigonometric Functions These functions provide scalar and SIMD version of the trigonometric functions in radians, degrees and $\pi$ multiples. ## Functions **Convenience header:** ### Radians [`eve::cos`](trigonometric/cos.html) : computes the cosine of its parameter [`eve::cot`](trigonometric/cot.html) : computes the cotangent of its parameter [`eve::csc`](trigonometric/csc.html) : computes the cosecant of its parameter [`eve::sec`](trigonometric/sec.html) : computes the secant of its parameter [`eve::sin`](trigonometric/sin.html) : computes the sine of its parameter [`eve::sinc`](trigonometric/sinc.html) : computes the cardinal sine of its parameter [`eve::sincos`](trigonometric/sincos.html) : computes the sine and cosine of its parameter [`eve::tan`](trigonometric/tan.html) : computes the tangent of its parameter ### Degrees [`eve::cosd`](trigonometric/cosd.html) : computes the cosine of its parameter [`eve::cotd`](trigonometric/cotd.html) : computes the cotangent of its parameter [`eve::cscd`](trigonometric/cscd.html) : computes the cosecant of its parameter [`eve::secd`](trigonometric/secd.html) : computes the secant of its parameter [`eve::sind`](trigonometric/sind.html) : computes the sine of its parameter [`eve::sindcosd`](trigonometric/sindcosd.html) : computes the sine and cosine of its parameter [`eve::tand`](trigonometric/tand.html) : computes the tangent of its parameter ### Multiple of $\pi$ [`eve::cospi`](trigonometric/cospi.html) : computes the cosine of its parameter [`eve::cotpi`](trigonometric/cotpi.html) : computes the cotangent of its parameter [`eve::cscpi`](trigonometric/cscpi.html) : computes the cosecant of its parameter [`eve::secpi`](trigonometric/secpi.html) : computes the secant of its parameter [`eve::sinpi`](trigonometric/sinpi.html) : computes the sine of its parameter [`eve::sinpic`](trigonometric/sincpi.html) : computes the cardinal sine of its parameter [`eve::sinpicospi`](trigonometric/sincospi.html) : computes the sine and cosine of its parameter [`eve::tanpi`](trigonometric/tanpi.html) : computes the tangent of its parameter
