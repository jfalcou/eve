**Expressive Vector Engine** (insert ../../crumbs.html here) # Exponential functions These functions provide scalar and SIMD version of exponential and logarithmic standard functions for floating point values. ## Functions **Convenience header:** [`eve::exp`](exponential/exp.html) : computes the exponential of its parameter ($e^x$) [`eve::exp10`](exponential/exp10.html) : computes the base 10 exponential of its parameter ($10^x$) [`eve::exp2`](exponential/exp2.html) : computes the base 2 exponential of its parameter ($2^x$) [`eve::expm1`](exponential/expm1.html) : computes the exponential of its parameters minus one with good accuracy ($e^x - 1$) [`eve::log`](exponential/log.html) : computes the natural logarithm of its parameter ($log(x)$) [`eve::log10`](exponential/log10.html) : computes the base 10 logarithm of its parameter ($log_{10}(x)$) [`eve::log2`](exponential/log2.html) : computes the base 2 logarithm of its parameter ($log_{2}(x)$) [`eve::log1p`](exponential/log1p.html) : computes the logarithm of its parameters plus one with good accuracy ($log(1+x)$) [`eve::logspace_add`](exponential/logspace_add.html) : computes the logarithm of a sum from the logarithms of the two terms [`eve::logspace_sub`](exponential/logspace_sub.html) : computes the logarithm of a differnce from the logarithms of the two terms [`eve::pow`](exponential/pow.html) : computes $x^y$ [`eve::pow_abs`](exponential/pow_abs.html) : computes $|x|^y$ [`eve::powm1`](exponential/powm1.html) : computes $x^y-1$ ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
